Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance to secure your preferred date and time. For peak periods (holidays, weekends) and larger groups, booking 1-2 weeks ahead is advisable. However, we do accommodate last-minute bookings when possible.

We accept all major credit and debit cards, PayPal, bank transfers, and cash payments (for certain services). Corporate accounts can be arranged with invoice payment terms for regular business clients.

For airport transfers, we monitor flight arrivals. Our drivers will wait for up to 60 minutes after the actual landing time at no extra charge. For longer delays, a small waiting fee may apply. Rest assured, we'll be there whenever you arrive.
