Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance to ensure availability, especially during peak times.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and cash payments for certain bookings.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, cancellations made 24+ hours before your journey are fully refundable. Later cancellations may incur a fee.

Do you provide child seats?

Yes, we can provide child seats upon request. Please specify in the special requirements field.
