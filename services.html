Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Find answers to our most commonly asked questions about our services.

How far in advance should I book a mini bus?

We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance for regular services and 4-6 weeks for peak times (holidays, wedding season, etc.) to ensure availability. For corporate clients with regular needs, we offer priority booking options.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

For airport transfers, we monitor all flight arrivals in real-time. Our drivers will adjust their schedule according to your actual arrival time at no extra charge for normal delays. We include a buffer in our pricing for this convenience.

Do you offer discounts for regular bookings?

Yes, we offer corporate accounts and contract rates for regular clients. Schools, sports teams, and businesses that use our services frequently can benefit from special pricing. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.

Are your drivers DBS checked?

Yes, all our drivers are fully DBS (Disclosure and Barring Service) checked, professionally licensed, and have extensive experience. We prioritize safety and professionalism, especially when transporting children and vulnerable adults.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made more than 48 hours before the scheduled service receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24-48 hours receive a 50% refund. Cancellations less than 24 hours before the service are non-refundable. Special terms may apply for corporate accounts.

Can I make multiple stops during my journey?

Yes, multiple pickup and drop-off points can be arranged for all our services. Please specify your requirements when booking so we can plan the route efficiently and provide accurate pricing. Additional stops may incur extra charges depending on the route.
