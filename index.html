Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to some of our most commonly asked questions.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance to ensure availability, especially during peak seasons. However, we can often accommodate last-minute bookings depending on our schedule.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 24 hours or more before the scheduled pickup time receive a full refund. Cancellations with less than 24 hours' notice may be subject to a cancellation fee.

Are your drivers professionally trained?

Yes, all our drivers are fully licensed professionals with extensive experience. They undergo regular training and hold all necessary certifications required by UK regulations.

Do you provide services outside of Coventry?

Absolutely! While we're based in Coventry, we offer our services throughout the UK for airport transfers, city tours, special events, and more.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, cash payments, and bank transfers. For corporate clients, we also offer invoicing options.
