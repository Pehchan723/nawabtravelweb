About Nawab Travel

Discover the story behind Coventry's best quality minibus service provider

Our Purpose

Our Mission

At Nawab Travel, our mission is to provide exceptional transportation services that exceed our customers' expectations. We are committed to safety, reliability, and comfort in every journey.

We believe that transportation should be stress-free, allowing our clients to focus on the purpose of their trip rather than the logistics of getting there.

"Our goal is to make every journey safe, comfortable, and memorable for our passengers."

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

These principles guide everything we do at Nawab Travel, from customer interactions to business decisions.

Safety First

We prioritize the safety of our passengers above all else. Our vehicles undergo regular maintenance checks, and our drivers are trained in defensive driving techniques.

Reliability

Our customers depend on us to be there when promised. We take pride in our punctuality and dependability, ensuring you never have to worry about transportation.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in every aspect of our service, from comfortable vehicles to professional drivers and responsive customer support available 24/7.
